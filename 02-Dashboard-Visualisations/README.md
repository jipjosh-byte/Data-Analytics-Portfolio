📊 **Spotify Music Streaming & Audio Feature Trends (Tableau)**


📌 **Project Overview**

Objective: To ingest multi-genre streaming data, evaluate market popularity distributions across tracks, and cross-examine acoustic song attributes to guide algorithmic content curation or playlisting strategies.

Organization Type: Music Streaming Platform / Digital Record Label / Entertainment Media Firm

Business Value: Enables A&R executives and playlist curators to dynamically optimize content length and acoustic features based on mathematical performance trends, maximizing listener retention and stream counts.

📊 **Core Data Insights (Soft Skills)**


The Popularity Hierarchy: Pop, Rap, and Rock strictly dominate track popularity within the dataset, massively outperforming niche formatting classes like Comedy or Opera.

The "Drake" Benchmark: Deep-dive identification isolates Drake as the standout individual artist, creating an overwhelming statistical lead over all other historical entries in the dataset.

Acoustic Correlations: Scatter trend analysis identifies a definitive positive correlation between track danceability and popularity weights—proving that increased rhythm profiles linearly drive audience streaming velocity.

The 5-Minute Threshold: Track length analysis displays a strict sweet spot between 3.5 and 4.5 minutes. Average popularity metrics collapse drastically for audio files dropping under or scaling past the 5-minute mark.

🛠️ **Tableau Techniques Deployed**


Custom horizontal bar-chart profiling with dynamic multi-color scale legends to rank tracking volume metrics.

Multidimensional box-and-whisker distribution matrices analyzing variance arrays for Acousticness, Danceability, and Energy attributes.

Linear trend-line modeling over scatter plots to formally isolate multi-variable relationships (Duration vs Popularity / Popularity vs Danceability).
