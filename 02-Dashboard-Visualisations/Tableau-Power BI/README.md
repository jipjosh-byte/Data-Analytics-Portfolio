# Spotify Music Streaming & Audio Feature Trends (Tableau)

## 📌 Project Overview
- **Objective:** To ingest multi-genre streaming data, evaluate market popularity distributions across tracks, and cross-examine acoustic song attributes to guide algorithmic content curation or playlisting strategies.
- **Organization Type:** Music Streaming Platform / Digital Record Label / Entertainment Media Firm
- **Business Value:** Enables A&R executives and playlist curators to dynamically optimize content length and acoustic features based on mathematical performance trends, maximizing listener retention and stream counts[cite: 4].

## 📊 Core Data Insights (Soft Skills)
- **The Popularity Hierarchy:** Pop, Rap, and Rock strictly dominate track popularity within the dataset, massively outperforming niche formatting classes like Comedy or Opera[cite: 4].
- **The "Drake" Benchmark:** Deep-dive identification isolates Drake as the standout individual artist, creating an overwhelming statistical lead over all other historical entries in the dataset[cite: 4].
- **Acoustic Correlations:** Scatter trend analysis identifies a definitive positive correlation between track danceability and popularity weights—proving that increased rhythm profiles linearly drive audience streaming velocity[cite: 4].
- **The 5-Minute Threshold:** Track length analysis displays a strict sweet spot between 3.5 and 4.5 minutes[cite: 4]. Average popularity metrics collapse drastically for audio files dropping under or scaling past the 5-minute mark[cite: 4].

## 🛠️ Tableau Techniques Deployed
- Custom horizontal bar-chart profiling with dynamic multi-color scale legends to rank tracking volume metrics[cite: 4].
- Multidimensional box-and-whisker distribution matrices analyzing variance arrays for Acousticness, Danceability, and Energy attributes[cite: 4].
- Linear trend-line modeling over scatter plots to formally isolate multi-variable relationships (Duration vs Popularity / Popularity vs Danceability)[cite: 4].
