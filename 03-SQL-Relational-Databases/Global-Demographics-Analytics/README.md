* [cite_start]**Business Question:** Which sovereign nation has the highest life expectancy to establish an internal healthcare benchmark? [cite: 1100]
* **SQL Approach:** Filtered missing inputs (`IS NOT NULL`), sorted parameters descending, and applied a row limit restriction (`LIMIT 1`).

* [cite_start]**Impact for Organization:** Stakeholders can isolate Andorra's systemic healthcare delivery framework to study it as a gold-standard model for other regional rollouts[cite: 1103].

### 2. High-Density Consumer Market Segmentation

* **SQL Approach:** Deployed a strict numeric comparative evaluation conditional filter directly on population attributes.

* [cite_start]**Business Question:** How do we programmatically verify and map the exact capital city name for specific sovereign states (e.g., Spain)? [cite: 1100]
* **SQL Approach:** Performed an inner `JOIN` between the `city` and `country` tables, matching the explicit `Capital` ID field to the unified city record.

* [cite_start]**Impact for Organization:** Ensures data integrity across international travel logs and corporate logistics pipelines, eliminating manual mapping errors[cite: 1103].

### 4. Regional Volatility & Wealth Tracking
* [cite_start]**Business Question:** Which target areas represent elite economic hubs when approximating per-capita wealth? [cite: 1100]

### 5. Granular Row Segmentation for Market Research

* **SQL Approach:** Combined `ORDER BY Population DESC` with a `LIMIT 10` and an explicit `OFFSET 30` constraint to skip the top 30 rows and pull the next 10.
